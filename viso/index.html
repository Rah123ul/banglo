Data-Backed Findings

Key Business Insights

🏆

Non-AC Taxi Highest Profit Per Ride

₹603.72 net per ride — the highest of all 4. Lower 20% commission vs 25% and lower fuel cost make it the most efficient vehicle despite modest fares.

📉

38% of Rides Never Complete

Only 61.91% of bookings succeed. Driver cancellations at 18% are the #1 failure — "more than permitted people" and "customer issues." Both operationally fixable.

⚡

Uber Go — Shortest Wait at 9.9 Min

Uber Go drivers wait just 9.9 min between orders and spend 83.5% of their shift driving — the highest utilisation rate across all 4 vehicles.

🕙

21:00 Is the Peak Revenue Hour

UberXL earns ₹8,10,247 at 21:00 — its single best hour. Go Sedan peaks at 07:00 (₹8,11,898). Night hours dominate revenue across all vehicles.

📍

Bellandur Tops UberXL Revenue

Bellandur generates ₹4,19,355 from 408 rides — highest-revenue pickup location for UberXL. Kudlu Gate leads Non-AC Taxi at ₹4,10,677.

💰

Uber Go Earns ₹2.33L / Month

Despite lower fares, 14 rides/day produces the highest monthly driver income at ₹2,33,288 — nearly double UberXL's ₹1,20,400 driver estimate.

Action Plan

Strategic Recommendations

⚡ Immediate — 0 to 30 Days

1

Deploy UberXL and Non-AC Taxi first — both scored 77/100 with the strongest profit margins

2

Position vehicles in Bellandur, Hebbal, Kudlu Gate — top revenue zones from dataset

3

Fix AC maintenance — "AC not working" is top customer cancel reason (403 UberXL cases)

4

Enforce passenger capacity — "More than permitted people" is #1 driver cancel reason

📈 Short-Term — 1 to 3 Months

1

Schedule UberXL shifts at 21:00 peak — highest revenue hour at ₹8.1L across fleet

2

Schedule Go Sedan from 07:00 — its peak hour with ₹8.12L total revenue

3

Improve driver ratings from 3.0 to 3.8+ — 48–49% of rides receive low ratings

4

Reduce vehicle breakdowns — top incomplete ride reason for UberXL and Non-AC Taxi

🏗 Long-Term — 3 to 12 Months

1

Scale only after success rate crosses 70% — all 4 currently at ~62%, below benchmark

2

Investigate Non-AC Taxi 59.06% margin — highest of all vehicles, best expansion candidate

3

Explore Uber Go volume — 14 rides/day and ₹2.33L/month makes it best driver income vehicle

4

Build monthly KPI dashboard tracking success rate, avg rating, net margin, rides/driver/day

Final Verdict: All 4 Vehicles Are Profitable ✅

The dataset confirms Bangalore is a commercially viable Uber market. Fleet revenue of ₹72.4M from just 4 vehicle types over 31 days proves strong demand. The 38% non-completion rate is the only major obstacle — fully fixable through operational changes, not market changes.
